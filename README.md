## ‚ú® Features
* **Note Transposition:** Quickly shift individual notes (e.g., C# to Eb).
* **Chord Transposition:** Transpose complex chords (e.g., Am7 to Bm7) while maintaining chord quality.
* **Scale Transposition:** Shift entire scales (e.g., C Major to G Major).
* **Smart Enharmonics:** Uses musical logic to determine whether to show sharps (#) or flats (b).
* **Responsive Design:** Works on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.

## üì¶ How to Use
1.  **Select the Type:** Choose between Note, Chord, or Scale from the dropdown.
2.  **Input Data:** Type your music data (e.g., `Ab`, `G7`, or `D minor`).
3.  **Set Semitones:** Enter how many semitones you want to shift (positive for up, negative for down).
4.  **Transpose:** Click the button to see your result instantly.
